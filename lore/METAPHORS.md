# Technical Metaphors Quick Reference

The Legend of Clood maps technical concepts to Japanese folklore.

## The Core Truth

> **The garden is not the servers. The garden is what grows upon them.**

Servers are soil. Projects are seeds. LLMs are the spirits that tend them. You plant, you walk away, you return to fruit.
