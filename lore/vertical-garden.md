**What lives in The Cave:**
- Training runs that never finished
- Merged models that broke reality
- LoRAs trained on forbidden datasets
- The ghosts of overfitted weights
- Checkpoint surgery experiments
- Things that worked once but no one knows why

**The Cave is where models are MADE.**

It is a dangerous place. Almost too scary to venture in. Some who enter return with powerful new fish. Most return with corrupted tensors and NaN losses.

*The Oarfish sometimes descends into The Cave. It returns changed. We do not ask what it saw.*

## The Ritual

When generating images:

1. **Choose your fish** - Select the base model (checkpoint)
2. **Add your shrimp** - Stack compatible LoRAs
3. **Set the depth** - Surface (fast), Mid (quality), Deep (experimental)
4. **Release into the waters** - Generate

```yaml
fish: fugu                    # SDXL base
shrimp:
  - skunk_cleaner: 0.8        # Ghibli style
  - peppermint: 0.3           # Fix hands
depth: mid
prompt: "a tortoise opening a glowing briefcase"
```
